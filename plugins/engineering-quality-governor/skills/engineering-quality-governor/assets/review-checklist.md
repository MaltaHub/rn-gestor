# Engineering Review Checklist

- The diff satisfies the stated objective.
- The scope stayed inside the diff contract.
- Architecture boundaries are preserved.
- Input validation and authorization are explicit where needed.
- Secrets and sensitive data are not exposed.
- Errors are handled with useful context.
- Tests cover the changed behavior at the right level.
- Performance impact is acceptable or measured.
- Rollback is possible and understood.
- Residual risks are documented.
